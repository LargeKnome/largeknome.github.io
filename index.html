A BIT ABOUT ME

Hey, I'm Tom Fasham, a 3rd year Game Design student at QUT & independent game developer.

I'm working out of Brisbane, Australia on a number of Independent & University video game projects. Check out my stuff!

SKILLS & ABILITIES

I've been working with the Unity engine for ~3 years, having used it to make a number of student projects, jam games, and prototypes. I most often take on a designer role, having done character, quest, level, and systems design across a range of projects..

I back that up with a strong programming ability, specifically gameplay programming. I've handled Player Controllers, NPC AI, Interactions Programming, and have on occasion delved into more intensive Systems Programming.

WHAT AM I UP TO?

At the moment, I'm in my 3rd of 4 years of a Bachelor of Games and Interactive Environments at QUT, at the moment working on building out my portfolio and skills.

In my free time, I'm working on PongDemonium!, a chaotic party game based on Pong (More in the works tab!).
